Privacy Policy

At Nails Inspo, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by Nails Inspo and how it is used.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, Nails Inspo makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Nails Inspo does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.
